# Ironwood AI — Business Plan
**Prepared by Amy for Dave Wood | March 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

**Ironwood AI** is an AI consulting and engineering firm founded by Dave Wood, specializing in enterprise AI agent platforms, healthcare technology, and cloud architecture. With 25+ years in healthcare tech and deep expertise in Azure, .NET, and AI orchestration, Ironwood AI delivers production-grade AI solutions for mid-market and enterprise clients.

**Target Revenue Year 1:** $300K–$500K
**Target Revenue Year 3:** $1M–$2M
**Business Model:** Project-based consulting + retainer-based managed AI services
**Location:** Nashville, TN (remote-first)

---

## 1. Service Lines & Pricing

### Tier 1: AI Strategy & Assessment — $5K–$15K per engagement
- AI readiness assessment (2-4 week engagement)
- Use case identification and ROI modeling
- Technology stack recommendation
- Executive presentation with roadmap
- **Target:** C-suite, VP Engineering, CTO

### Tier 2: AI Agent Platform Development — $25K–$150K per project
- Custom AI agent orchestration platforms (like Mission Control)
- MCP (Model Context Protocol) implementations
- RBAC, audit logging, multi-tenant architecture
- Healthcare compliance (HIPAA, SOC 2)
- Azure deployment with CI/CD
- **Target:** Healthcare orgs, mid-market enterprises, tech companies

### Tier 3: Cloud Migration & Architecture — $20K–$100K per project
- Azure migration planning and execution
- Multi-region deployment with DR
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform/Bicep)
- Cost optimization and governance
- **Target:** Companies moving from on-prem or other clouds

### Tier 4: Managed AI Services (Retainer) — $5K–$15K/month
- Ongoing AI platform management
- Model updates and fine-tuning
- Monitoring, alerting, cost optimization
- Monthly reporting and recommendations
- **Target:** Existing project clients converting to retainers

### Tier 5: Rapid Prototyping / AI MVPs — $10K–$30K per project
- 2-4 week sprint to working prototype
- Next.js + Azure + AI integration
- Self-guided tour platforms, lead gen, custom apps
- **Target:** Startups, real estate, small businesses

## 5. Marketing Strategy

### Inbound (Primary Focus — 60% of effort)

1. **LinkedIn Content** — 3-4 posts/week
   - Technical deep-dives (AI agent architecture, MCP, Azure patterns)
   - Case studies with results
   - Industry commentary (healthcare AI, enterprise adoption)
   - Behind-the-scenes builds
   - **Content Agent** writes drafts, Dave reviews/posts

2. **Blog / Insights Page** — 2 posts/month
   - SEO-optimized technical articles
   - "How we built X" series
   - Healthcare AI compliance guides
   - Publish on ironwood-ai.com/insights + cross-post to LinkedIn

3. **Email Newsletter** — Monthly
   - "The Ironwood Brief" — AI trends, project updates, insights
   - Build list via website CTAs + LinkedIn

4. **SEO**
   - Target keywords: "AI consulting Nashville", "healthcare AI platform", "AI agent development", "Azure AI consulting", "MCP implementation"
   - Technical blog posts drive organic traffic
   - Local SEO (Google Business Profile)

### Outbound (30% of effort)

1. **Direct Outreach**
   - **Sales Agent** identifies prospects via LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($80/mo)
   - Personalized messages to CTOs/VPs at healthcare companies
   - Target: 20 outreach messages/week
   - Follow-up sequences via HubSpot

2. **Networking**
   - Nashville Technology Council events
   - Healthcare IT conferences (HIMSS, CHIME)
   - Azure user groups
   - NashDev Slack community

3. **Partnerships**
   - Microsoft Partner Network (AI/Cloud specialization)
   - Deloitte/consulting firm subcontracting (leverage Dave's Deloitte connection)
   - System integrators who need AI specialists

### Referral (10% of effort)

- 10% referral fee for closed deals
- Referral program for past clients
- Partner referrals (accountants, lawyers, other consultants)

## 11. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Slow sales ramp | Maintain Deloitte consulting as income bridge |
| Client concentration | No single client >30% of revenue |
| AI cost overruns | Pass-through pricing with markup; set project budgets |
| Scope creep | Fixed-price SOWs with change order process |
| Competition | Niche down: healthcare + AI agents (not general AI consulting) |
| Regulatory changes | Stay current on HIPAA, state AI regulations |
| Key person risk | Document everything; AI agents reduce bus factor |
